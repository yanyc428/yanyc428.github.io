I am a Ph.D. student at the [College of Computer Science and Technology](https://www.cs.zju.edu.cn/), **Zhejiang University**, where I am advised by Prof. Yongliang Shen and Prof. Weiming Lu. I expect to graduate in 2027.

My research focuses on **large language model (LLM) reasoning** and **AI agents**. I am particularly interested in how to make reasoning models *think* more effectively and efficiently — including long-horizon and infinite-horizon reasoning, reinforcement learning for reasoning, reward modeling and verification, and agents that act reliably in real environments.

I am currently a research intern, and I have worked on projects spanning mathematical reasoning, tool use, and GUI / mobile agents. You can find my work on [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=NcFV4NAAAAAJ), [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/author/Yuchen-Yan/2284984220), and [OpenReview](https://openreview.net/profile?id=~Yuchen_Yan4).

Feel free to reach out at **yanyuchen@zju.edu.cn** if you would like to discuss research or potential collaborations.

## Research Interests

- **LLM Reasoning** — long-horizon reasoning, efficient / adaptive reasoning, chain-of-thought tuning
- **Reinforcement Learning for LLMs** — reward modeling, verification, policy optimization
- **AI Agents** — tool use, GUI and mobile agents, agents grounded in environments
