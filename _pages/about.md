I am a Ph.D. student in **Artificial Intelligence** at **Zhejiang University**, where I am a member of [REAL Lab](https://zju-real.github.io), advised by Dr. Yongliang Shen and Dr. Jian Shao. I expect to graduate around 2027. Before that, I received my B.S. from Beijing Normal University.

My research centers on **large language model (LLM) reasoning** and **AI agents**, with a focus on how to make reasoning models think more effectively and efficiently. My work spans long-horizon and infinite-horizon reasoning, reinforcement learning for reasoning, reward modeling and verification, and agents that act reliably in real environments. To date I have published 15 CCF-A papers, including 8 as first or co-first author.

Alongside my Ph.D., I have worked as an intern on foundation-model teams in industry, including Meituan (LongCat), Ant Group (Ling / Ring), and currently Tencent (Hunyuan), on large-scale pre-training, post-training, and reinforcement learning for reasoning models.

You can find my work on [Google Scholar](https://scholar.google.com/citations?user=NcFV4NAAAAAJ), [Semantic Scholar](https://www.semanticscholar.org/author/Yuchen-Yan/2284984220), and [OpenReview](https://openreview.net/profile?id=~Yuchen_Yan4). Feel free to reach out at **yanyuchen@zju.edu.cn** if you would like to discuss research or potential collaborations.

## Research Interests

- **LLM Reasoning**: long-horizon and infinite-horizon reasoning, efficient and adaptive reasoning, chain-of-thought tuning
- **Reinforcement Learning for LLMs**: reward modeling, verification, policy optimization
- **AI Agents**: tool use, GUI and mobile agents, agents grounded in environments
